About Us!

Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide children with an excellent learning environment where they have an opportunity to develop skills which is needed to become firm believers,This is our commitment to the parents, who entrust us with the responsibility of educating & nurturing their young ones.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to child centric encouraging Activity based learning. In our curriculum the student is the primary focus and each child is involved in the learning process. Students are continually encouraged to participate them to develop an active respect for the environment.

Our Goals

We strive to provide an environment that is basically sacred, and has a framework of discipline that is flexible yet supportive, a freedom that allows students to learn even from their mistakes, to develop into person moulded by the correlatives of freedom and responsibility.

Why Choose Us!

The practical experience of learning enables our children to leave our school daily with either something in their hand, something in their mind and especially something in their heart

The school is striving hard to make the best possible efforts to inculcate strong values combining with academics and extra-curricular activities in the children.

While academic excellence is our major thrust, the school is also devoted to prepare the students for life, groom them to face the challenges of tomorrow, and encourage them to be socially relevant.
